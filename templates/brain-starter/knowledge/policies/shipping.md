## Tracking workflow

1. Check ecommerce order status.
2. Check logistics tracking.
3. If both agree, draft customer update.
4. If systems disagree, escalate.

## Known exceptions

Document carriers, regions, bulky/chilled/fragile items, customs, pre-orders, or seasonal delays.
