# Email Sequence Prompt

```text
Write a complete email sequence.

Audience: {{audience}}
Offer: {{offer}}
Objective: {{objective}}
Objections: {{objections}}
Voice: {{voice}}

For each email provide purpose, send timing, 3 subject lines, preview text, body, and one CTA. Address objections explicitly and avoid fake urgency. End with a timing and measurement table.
```
