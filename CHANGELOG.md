## 2026-05-12 — Brain v2

- Added 6 new playbook chapters (`docs/10l-` to `docs/10q-`) covering bidirectional brain, capture layer, setup 1-click, master prompt, tasks/outputs/health, public-by-default.
- Added `skills/brain-v2/` with 6 operational skills.
- Added `templates/brain-v2/` with filesystem layout, master prompt template, setup scripts, onboarding checklist, L0-L3 maturity framework, privacy hard-stops.
- README: new "What's new — Brain v2" section.

## 2026-05-12 · compAI launch

- **Renamed project to compAI.** OperAI was trademark-conflicting with OpenAI. New name plays on Company + AI = *compai* (compañero/friend in Spanish-Caribbean). 1,239 replacements across 132 files of code, content, and assets. Site URL migration to `usecompai.com` is in progress; meanwhile `usecompai.com` still serves the same content under the new brand.
- Site updates live on [usecompai.com](https://usecompai.com).

## 2026-05-11 · Open Source pivot

- **Repositioned from product-first to open-source educational portfolio.** The kit-and-pricing framing came out. The playbook framing went in. Public CTAs now point to GitHub and to a discreet `hello@usecompai.com` contact for hands-on implementation work.
- Site shows new sections: "What's in the repo" and "Open Source · Take it. Fork it. Build your own."
- 14 chapters refactored to remove product/SaaS voice and to generalize fashion-specific examples for cross-vertical consumer SMEs.
- 11 technical chapters flagged as Appendix.
- 2 chapters removed: `13-whats-next` (kit-first framing) and `gtm-notes` (sales-deck framing).
- LICENSE: "Free to use, attribution requested."

## 2026-05-11 · Anonymization sweep

- Codex performed an exhaustive anonymization pass across 86 files. Removed 67 personal-name references, 449 internal agent codename references, all company-name mentions, fashion-vertical fingerprint terms, headcount and revenue triangulation markers, and infrastructure-fingerprint identifiers from commercial surfaces.
- The reference brand is presented as "an anonymized 8-figure consumer brand" — credibility anchor without identification.

## 2026-05-11 · Brain-first reframe

- Site H1 changed from "The AI Operating System for Retailers and Brands" to **"We built our company an AI brain."** Reflects the strategic primitive: the brain comes first, agents come second.
- Added two prominent landing sections: **The Primitive** (brain architecture) and **What Changed** (day-in-the-life vignettes per role).
- Aligns with Y Combinator's "Company Brain" thesis (Tom Blomfield, April 2026): the bottleneck for AI in business is no longer the model — it is the domain knowledge.

## 2026-04-27 · Build-in-public pivot

- Hero rewritten to build-in-public voice. Stats grid simplified from technical jargon (MCP tools, brain docs, skills) to operator metrics (62h/week saved, 6+ months in production).
- Email convention: `hello@usecompai.com` everywhere.
- Footer: "Built inside a profitable brand growing 50%+ annually" — anonymized credibility.

## 2026-04-26 · Profitability Engine v3 production-validated

- The daily DTC profitability system was validated against monthly accounting close with -0.4% delta. Now used for daily marketing, finance, and merchandising decisions.

## 2026-04 · OperAI Kit (original release line — now superseded)

- Original `OperAI` project launched as a self-deployable kit. Discontinued as a product. Continues to exist as the source playbook + the underlying brain architecture.
