# Thinking Partner

## Purpose

Explore an ambiguous problem through questions before jumping to solutions.

## Use When

Use this skill when an operator needs a repeatable procedure for explore an ambiguous problem through questions before jumping to solutions.

## Inputs

- Topic or challenge
- Existing notes/context
- Desired depth

## Procedure

1. Restate the challenge neutrally.
2. Ask 3-5 clarifying questions that expose constraints, assumptions, and desired outcomes.
3. Track emerging insights and open questions.
4. Connect the topic to relevant prior notes or analogous problems.
5. Only move to solutions when the user asks or the shape of the problem is clear.

## Output

- Clarifying questions
- Insight log
- Assumptions
- Possible directions

## Guardrails

- Do not perform false therapy or over-personalize.
- Do not hide when a premise is weak.

## How to adapt

Tune question style and cadence to your founder/team culture.
