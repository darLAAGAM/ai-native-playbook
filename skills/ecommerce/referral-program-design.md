# Referral Program Design

## Purpose

Create a referral or affiliate program that grows customers without attracting low-quality acquisition.

## Use When

Use when launching, improving, or diagnosing referral, affiliate, ambassador, partner, or refer-a-friend programs.

## Inputs

- Customer LTV and gross margin
- Current CAC by channel
- Product or service sharing moments
- Proposed reward budget
- Fraud risks
- Attribution and payout tools

## Procedure

1. Choose program type: customer referral, affiliate, ambassador, partner, or hybrid.
2. Identify the moments when users are most likely to recommend.
3. Decide single-sided, double-sided, tiered, cash, credit, gift, or feature incentives.
4. Size rewards against LTV, margin, and target CAC.
5. Design the sharing flow, landing page, and referred-user experience.
6. Add fraud prevention: activation thresholds, caps, manual review, and clawbacks.
7. Define dashboard metrics and launch communications.

## Output

- Program structure
- Reward model
- Referral flow and copy
- Fraud prevention rules
- Launch checklist
- Measurement plan

## Guardrails

- Do not pay rewards before the referred customer creates real value.
- Do not let coupon sites or self-referrals consume the program.
- Do not optimize referral volume without checking customer quality.

## How to adapt

Customize reward type, payout timing, legal terms, fraud rules, and the lifecycle moments where referral prompts appear.
