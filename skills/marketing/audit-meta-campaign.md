# Audit Meta Campaigns

## Purpose

Audit paid social campaigns for fatigue, overlap, tracking quality, and scaling opportunities.

## Use When

Use this skill when an operator needs a repeatable procedure for audit paid social campaigns for fatigue, overlap, tracking quality, and scaling opportunities.

## Inputs

- Campaign/ad set/ad export
- At least 14 days of performance
- Pixel/CAPI status if available
- Targets or budget

## Procedure

1. Check tracking health: pixel, server events, event match quality, prioritized events, and conversion mapping.
2. Score creative fatigue using frequency, CTR trend, CPM trend, engagement decay, and CPA movement.
3. Find audience overlap or fragmentation across ad sets.
4. Evaluate structure: prospecting vs retargeting, broad vs interest, Advantage+ vs manual, budget size vs learning needs.
5. Rank actions: kill, refresh, consolidate, scale, or monitor.

## Output

- Account health score
- Fatigue alerts
- Audience issues
- Tracking gaps
- Scaling candidates
- Priority actions

## Guardrails

- Use at least 14 days for trend claims.
- Do not scale a campaign on too little conversion volume.
- State when tracking gaps make ROAS unreliable.

## How to adapt

Adapt fatigue thresholds to your category, AOV, conversion lag, and creative production cadence.
