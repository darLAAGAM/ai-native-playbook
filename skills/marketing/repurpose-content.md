# Repurpose Content Across Channels

## Purpose

Turn one strong piece of content into channel-native posts, email snippets, ad hooks, and short video scripts.

## Use When

Use when you need to turn one strong piece of content into channel-native posts, email snippets, ad hooks, and short video scripts.

## Inputs

- Source article/transcript/post
- Target platforms
- Brand voice
- Goal

## Procedure

1. Extract the core message, strongest proof, and 3-5 reusable angles.
2. Choose platform-native formats rather than shrinking the same text everywhere.
3. Write derivatives for selected channels: social posts, thread, newsletter teaser, ad hooks, short video script.
4. Preserve factual claims and source meaning.
5. Add publishing notes and test hypotheses for each derivative.

## Output

- Source analysis
- Content package by channel
- Hooks
- CTA variants
- Testing notes

## Guardrails

- Do not invent claims, quotes, or results.
- Do not use the same hook everywhere if platform context differs.

## How to adapt

Replace platform list, tone, forbidden phrases, and approval workflow.
