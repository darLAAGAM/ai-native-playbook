# Landing Page Message Match Audit

## Purpose

Check whether a landing page delivers what the ad, email, or post promised.

## Use When

Use before launching campaigns, when conversion rate drops, or when ads get clicks but the landing page underperforms.

## Inputs

- Traffic source and campaign objective
- Ad or email copy
- Landing page URL, screenshot, or page copy
- Target audience
- Offer
- Conversion action

## Procedure

1. Extract the promise, audience, offer, proof, and CTA from the traffic source.
2. Compare the page headline, first screen, CTA, and proof against that promise.
3. Flag mismatches in language, offer, audience, urgency, or expected next step.
4. Review above-the-fold clarity on mobile and desktop.
5. Check form or checkout friction relative to intent level.
6. Recommend copy, layout, proof, and CTA fixes in priority order.
7. Convert the top issues into test hypotheses if there is enough traffic.

## Output

- Message match score
- Mismatch list
- Priority fixes
- Form or checkout friction notes
- Test hypotheses

## Guardrails

- Do not optimize the page without considering the traffic source.
- Do not add proof or urgency that is not true.
- Do not judge desktop only if mobile traffic is meaningful.

## How to adapt

Add your page templates, brand voice, compliance constraints, and minimum proof standards.
