# Weekly Marketing Performance Summary

## Purpose

Produce a plain-English weekly readout of what changed, why it matters, and what to do next.

## Use When

Use this skill when an operator needs a repeatable procedure for produce a plain-English weekly readout of what changed, why it matters, and what to do next.

## Inputs

- Spend/revenue/conversion data
- Prior week
- Trailing 4-week average
- Targets

## Procedure

1. Compare each channel against prior week and trailing average.
2. Ignore tiny changes unless they move profit, revenue, CAC, or learning velocity.
3. Identify the top 3 fires, top 3 wins, and housekeeping items.
4. Explain likely drivers with evidence and confidence.
5. Recommend next-week actions with owner, expected impact, and watch metric.

## Output

- Executive summary
- Fires
- Wins
- Channel table
- Actions for next week

## Guardrails

- Do not over-explain every metric.
- State attribution limitations.
- Call out missing data early.

## How to adapt

Adapt metrics to your channel mix: paid, email, SEO, retail, marketplace, affiliates, or wholesale.
