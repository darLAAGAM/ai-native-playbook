# Find Wasted Ad Spend

## Purpose

Identify spend leaking into non-converting terms, placements, audiences, or creatives.

## Use When

Use when you need to identify spend leaking into non-converting terms, placements, audiences, or creatives.

## Inputs

- Search term report
- Placement report
- Audience/ad performance
- Conversion and spend targets

## Procedure

1. Set a meaningful spend threshold based on CPA target and conversion lag.
2. Find terms, placements, audiences, or ads with spend above threshold and zero or weak conversions.
3. Group wasted spend by theme: wrong intent, irrelevant geography, competitor ambiguity, low-quality placement, creative fatigue, or tracking issue.
4. Build negative keyword, placement exclusion, pause, or restructure lists.
5. Estimate recoverable monthly spend.

## Output

- Waste categories
- Evidence table
- Recommended exclusions
- Pause/restructure list
- Estimated savings

## Guardrails

- Do not exclude early-learning data before conversion lag has passed.
- Check assisted conversions before cutting high-funnel assets.

## How to adapt

Set thresholds from your CPA target, sales cycle, and attribution model.
