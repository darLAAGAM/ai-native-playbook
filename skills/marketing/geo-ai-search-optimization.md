# GEO AI Search Optimization

## Purpose

Optimize content so AI search systems can parse, cite, and summarize the business accurately.

## Use When

Use when you need to optimize content so AI search systems can parse, cite, and summarize the business accurately.

## Inputs

- Target page or entity
- Target questions
- Competitor set
- Current content

## Procedure

1. Create a clear first-paragraph entity definition: what it is, who it serves, and what it does.
2. Add quotable facts, specific numbers, dates where relevant, and sourceable claims.
3. Build FAQ sections around how customers actually ask questions.
4. Use tables for comparisons and decision criteria.
5. Add freshness signals, author/company credentials, and schema where appropriate.
6. Test representative prompts in AI search tools and record mention/citation outcomes.

## Output

- GEO score
- Content changes
- FAQ/questions
- Schema recommendations
- Monitoring queries

## Guardrails

- Do not make unsupported claims for citation bait.
- Keep tone factual, not hype-driven.

## How to adapt

Customize target prompts, competitor names, and claims your business can substantiate.
