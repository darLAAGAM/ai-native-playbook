# Write Email Sequence

## Purpose

Draft a complete lifecycle or promotional email sequence with timing, subject lines, body copy, and CTAs.

## Use When

Use this skill when an operator needs a repeatable procedure for draft a complete lifecycle or promotional email sequence with timing, subject lines, body copy, and CTAs.

## Inputs

- Audience
- Offer
- Objective
- Objections
- Brand voice
- Timing constraints

## Procedure

1. Define the sequence goal and conversion event.
2. Map each email to one purpose: welcome, educate, proof, objection, offer, urgency, or reactivation.
3. Choose copy frameworks per email: AIDA, PAS, BAB, proof, or direct offer.
4. Write 3 subject line options, preview text, body, and one CTA per email.
5. Check voice consistency and remove generic AI phrasing.
6. Add measurement plan: open, click, conversion, unsubscribe, revenue per recipient.

## Output

- Sequence overview
- Email drafts
- Timing table
- Voice notes
- Measurement plan

## Guardrails

- One primary CTA per email.
- No fake urgency.
- Respect consent, unsubscribe, and regional email law.

## How to adapt

Add your brand voice rules, legal footer needs, discount policy, and sending cadence.
