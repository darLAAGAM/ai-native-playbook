# Cash Runway Check

## Purpose

Estimate how many months the business can operate under base, downside, and aggressive scenarios.

## Use When

Use this skill when an operator needs a repeatable procedure for estimate how many months the business can operate under base, downside, and aggressive scenarios.

## Inputs

- Current cash
- Accounts receivable/payable
- Monthly fixed costs
- Variable margin
- Forecast assumptions

## Procedure

1. Reconcile current cash and near-term obligations.
2. Calculate average monthly net burn or cash generation using the last 3 months and current run-rate.
3. Build base, downside, and upside scenarios for revenue, margin, ad spend, payroll, and inventory buys.
4. Estimate runway in months and identify covenant, tax, payroll, and inventory cliffs.
5. Recommend immediate actions if runway is below the company threshold.

## Output

- Runway by scenario
- Main assumptions
- Cash cliffs
- Recommended actions
- Confidence

## Guardrails

- Show ranges, not a single false-precision number.
- Separate cash timing from accounting profit.
- Escalate if payroll or tax obligations are at risk.

## How to adapt

Set thresholds for minimum cash, inventory purchasing cycles, and required owner approval.
