# Sell-Through Review

## Purpose

Identify products or categories that are overperforming, underperforming, or at risk of stockout/overstock.

## Use When

Use when you need to identify products or categories that are overperforming, underperforming, or at risk of stockout/overstock.

## Inputs

- Sales units by SKU/category
- Inventory on hand
- Launch date
- Initial buy
- Price/markdown status

## Procedure

1. Calculate sell-through, weeks of cover, sales velocity, stockout risk, and aged stock.
2. Segment by product/category, size/variant, channel, and location where available.
3. Classify items: reorder/transfer, protect margin, promote, markdown, hold, or discontinue.
4. Explain drivers: price, seasonality, broken size curve, poor visibility, channel mismatch, or demand signal.
5. Recommend actions with quantity and deadline when possible.

## Output

- SKU/category status table
- Risks
- Recommended actions
- Confidence

## Guardrails

- Do not markdown items that are inventory constrained.
- Do not reorder based on one-day spikes without checking trend and lead time.

## How to adapt

Replace SKU attributes with your product taxonomy and inventory lead times.
