# Leave Coverage Plan

## Purpose

Plan coverage for vacations, parental leave, sick leave, or temporary absence.

## Use When

Use this skill when an operator needs a repeatable procedure for plan coverage for vacations, parental leave, sick leave, or temporary absence.

## Inputs

- Leave dates
- Role responsibilities
- Current projects
- Backup candidates
- Critical deadlines

## Procedure

1. List responsibilities that must continue during absence.
2. Classify work: pause, delegate, automate, or escalate.
3. Assign backups and define decision rights.
4. Create handover checklist: access, docs, recurring tasks, unresolved issues, emergency contacts.
5. Schedule pre-leave and return handoff.

## Output

- Coverage map
- Handover checklist
- Decision rights
- Risk list

## Guardrails

- Respect privacy: do not disclose medical or personal details.
- Do not rely on one backup for every critical process.

## How to adapt

Add local HR rules, approval paths, and team-specific recurring tasks.
