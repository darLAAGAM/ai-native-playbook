# Candidate Scorecard

## Purpose

Evaluate candidates consistently against role outcomes instead of vibes.

## Use When

Use this skill when an operator needs a repeatable procedure for evaluate candidates consistently against role outcomes instead of vibes.

## Inputs

- Role outcomes
- Must-have skills
- Interview notes
- Work sample or case if available

## Procedure

1. Convert role needs into 5-7 evaluation criteria.
2. Score each criterion with evidence from notes or work sample.
3. Separate demonstrated evidence from interviewer inference.
4. Identify risks, missing evidence, and follow-up questions.
5. Recommend hire, no-hire, hold, or additional interview with confidence.

## Output

- Scorecard
- Evidence notes
- Risks
- Follow-up questions
- Recommendation

## Guardrails

- Avoid protected attributes and personality stereotypes.
- Do not average away a critical must-have failure.

## How to adapt

Define your role rubric, bar raiser rules, and compensation/leveling process.
