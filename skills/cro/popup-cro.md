# Popup CRO

## Purpose

Design popups, modals, banners, and slide-ins that convert without damaging the user experience.

## Use When

Use for newsletter capture, lead magnets, discounts, announcements, exit intent saves, feedback prompts, or conversion banners.

## Inputs

- Popup goal
- Target audience and traffic source
- Current trigger and frequency
- Offer or message
- Desktop and mobile constraints
- Compliance requirements

## Procedure

1. Define the popup's job and what user behavior should trigger it.
2. Choose the least intrusive format that can still achieve the goal.
3. Match offer and copy to page context and visitor intent.
4. Set targeting, exclusions, frequency cap, and cool-down rules.
5. Write headline, supporting copy, CTA, and neutral dismiss text.
6. Check accessibility: focus trap, keyboard dismissal, screen reader behavior, and contrast.
7. Define metrics and A/B tests.

## Output

- Popup strategy
- Trigger and targeting rules
- Full copy
- Design and mobile notes
- Measurement plan

## Guardrails

- Do not show intrusive popups during checkout or critical flows.
- Do not hide dismiss controls.
- Do not use guilt-based or misleading decline copy.

## How to adapt

Customize offers, traffic segments, consent language, brand tone, and popup frequency policy.
