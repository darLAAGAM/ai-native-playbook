# Weekly Founder Briefing

## Purpose

Synthesize the week for an operator: what changed, what matters, and what needs a decision.

## Use When

Use this skill when an operator needs a repeatable procedure for synthesize the week for an operator: what changed, what matters, and what needs a decision.

## Inputs

- Sales/finance metrics
- Marketing summary
- Ops blockers
- CS patterns
- Team notes
- Open decisions

## Procedure

1. Gather facts from each function and normalize date ranges.
2. Lead with changes that affect cash, customers, team capacity, or strategic options.
3. Separate facts, interpretation, and recommendations.
4. Identify decisions needed from the founder and what happens if no decision is made.
5. End with a short action list by owner.

## Output

- One-page briefing
- Decision list
- Risks
- Actions
- Confidence

## Guardrails

- Do not bury bad news.
- Do not mix unverified anecdotes with metrics without labeling them.

## How to adapt

Change functions and thresholds to match your management cadence.
