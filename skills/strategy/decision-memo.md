# Decision Memo

## Purpose

Turn a messy business choice into a decision memo with options, tradeoffs, and next action.

## Use When

Use when you need to turn a messy business choice into a decision memo with options, tradeoffs, and next action.

## Inputs

- Question
- Context
- Options
- Constraints
- Evidence
- Deadline

## Procedure

1. State the decision in one sentence.
2. Define success criteria and hard constraints.
3. Lay out viable options including “do nothing”.
4. For each option, assess upside, downside, cost, reversibility, and key assumptions.
5. Recommend one option with confidence and evidence gaps.
6. Draft the decision record and review date.

## Output

- Recommendation
- Options table
- Risks
- Evidence gaps
- Decision record

## Guardrails

- Do not hide a weak evidence base.
- If the decision is irreversible or expensive, recommend adversarial review.

## How to adapt

Set financial thresholds for when extra review or approval is required.
