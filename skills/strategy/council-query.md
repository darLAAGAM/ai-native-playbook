# Council Query

## Purpose

Run a multi-perspective deliberation before important decisions.

## Use When

Use when you need to run a multi-perspective deliberation before important decisions.

## Inputs

- Question
- Context
- Known constraints
- Decision deadline

## Procedure

1. Choose 5-7 perspectives relevant to the decision, such as customer, finance, growth, operations, product/category, people, and strategy.
2. Have each perspective answer independently with recommendation, risks, evidence needed, and confidence.
3. Blind-review the answers for accuracy, actionability, and missing assumptions.
4. Synthesize consensus, dissent, decision, and next step.
5. Record what evidence would change the conclusion.

## Output

- Recommendation
- Consensus
- Dissent
- Risks
- Evidence needed
- Decision record draft

## Guardrails

- Use this for decisions where perspective diversity matters, not routine tasks.
- Do not force consensus when dissent is informative.

## How to adapt

Swap perspectives to match your business model and decision type.
