# Supplier Follow-Up

## Purpose

Draft precise supplier follow-ups that get dates, quantities, documents, and blockers resolved.

## Use When

Use when you need to draft precise supplier follow-ups that get dates, quantities, documents, and blockers resolved.

## Inputs

- Supplier name
- PO/order details
- Current issue
- Required deadline
- Prior thread if available

## Procedure

1. Clarify the objective: ETA, quantity confirmation, document, quality issue, payment status, or escalation.
2. List factual context: PO, SKU/item, quantity, promised date, current gap.
3. Ask for a specific response format and deadline.
4. If risk is high, include impact and escalation path.
5. Produce both external email and internal note.

## Output

- Supplier email
- Internal summary
- Requested fields
- Follow-up deadline

## Guardrails

- Avoid vague “please update” messages.
- Do not threaten escalation unless the business is ready to act.

## How to adapt

Add your supplier tone, escalation rules, and required document checklist.
