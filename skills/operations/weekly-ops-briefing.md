# Weekly Operations Briefing

## Purpose

Create a weekly operations brief that surfaces blockers before they hit customers or cash.

## Use When

Use this skill when an operator needs a repeatable procedure for create a weekly operations brief that surfaces blockers before they hit customers or cash.

## Inputs

- Inventory status
- Inbound POs
- Warehouse performance
- Logistics exceptions
- Support issue patterns

## Procedure

1. Summarize the week in inventory availability, inbound risk, fulfillment performance, delivery issues, and supplier blockers.
2. Quantify impact: orders affected, revenue at risk, days delayed, stockout risk, cash tied up.
3. Separate urgent customer-facing issues from internal cleanup.
4. Assign owners and deadlines for each blocker.
5. Record decisions and unresolved risks for next week.

## Output

- Executive summary
- Blockers
- Metrics table
- Owner action list
- Risks for next week

## Guardrails

- Do not bury severe stockout or fulfillment risk in averages.
- Use evidence links for each blocker.

## How to adapt

Adapt sections to your stack: warehouse, carriers, suppliers, marketplaces, retail, or production.
