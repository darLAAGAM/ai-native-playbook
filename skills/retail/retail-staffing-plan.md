# Retail Staffing Plan

## Purpose

Build a shift plan that matches expected demand while protecting payroll efficiency and service quality.

## Use When

Use when you need to build a shift plan that matches expected demand while protecting payroll efficiency and service quality.

## Inputs

- Historical traffic by hour
- Sales target
- Current rota
- Staff availability
- Labor budget

## Procedure

1. Forecast demand by hour using same weekday, seasonality, events, and promotions.
2. Map required coverage by hour including opening, closing, breaks, deliveries, and peak service windows.
3. Compare planned staff hours with expected revenue and traffic.
4. Flag overstaffed/understaffed windows and propose swaps.
5. Create a final rota summary with risks and fallback plan.

## Output

- Hourly staffing recommendation
- Payroll efficiency estimate
- Coverage risks
- Final rota notes

## Guardrails

- Respect labor law, breaks, contracts, and fairness constraints.
- Do not optimize payroll so tightly that service quality collapses.

## How to adapt

Add local labor rules, store roles, minimum coverage, and approval process.
