# Store Traffic And Conversion Review

## Purpose

Diagnose whether retail performance is driven by footfall, conversion, basket size, staffing, or stock.

## Use When

Use this skill when an operator needs a repeatable procedure for diagnose whether retail performance is driven by footfall, conversion, basket size, staffing, or stock.

## Inputs

- Traffic by hour/day
- POS sales
- Transactions
- Staffing schedule
- Stock availability

## Procedure

1. Calculate traffic, conversion rate, AOV, UPT, revenue per visitor, and revenue per staff hour.
2. Compare to prior week, same weekday, and target.
3. Identify the bottleneck: low traffic, low conversion, low basket, understaffing, overstaffing, or stock gap.
4. Segment by hour to see if staffing matched demand.
5. Recommend actions: rota change, VM/product focus, local marketing, stock transfer, training, or promo support.

## Output

- Performance driver diagnosis
- Hourly table
- Bottleneck
- Recommended actions

## Guardrails

- Do not blame store teams when traffic or stock explains the miss.
- Check data completeness from counters and POS.

## How to adapt

Tune benchmarks by location, day type, season, and store format.
