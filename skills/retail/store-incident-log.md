# Store Incident Log

## Purpose

Convert messy store incidents into structured records that operations can resolve and learn from.

## Use When

Use this skill when an operator needs a repeatable procedure for convert messy store incidents into structured records that operations can resolve and learn from.

## Inputs

- Incident message/photos
- Store/location
- Time
- Staff member or manager note

## Procedure

1. Classify incident: customer, safety, facilities, POS, inventory, staffing, loss, delivery, or other.
2. Record facts: what happened, when, impact, immediate action taken, and evidence.
3. Assign severity and owner.
4. Define next action and deadline.
5. If recurring, link to previous incidents and suggest a root-cause review.

## Output

- Structured incident record
- Severity
- Owner
- Next action
- Follow-up date

## Guardrails

- Protect personal data and sensitive employee/customer details.
- Escalate safety issues immediately.

## How to adapt

Adapt severity levels, owners, and incident categories to your stores.
