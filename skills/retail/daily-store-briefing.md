# Daily Store Briefing

## Purpose

Prepare a concise store briefing for the manager before the trading day starts.

## Use When

Use when you need to prepare a concise store briefing for the manager before the trading day starts.

## Inputs

- Yesterday sales
- Today target
- Staff roster
- Stock alerts
- Local events/weather if relevant

## Procedure

1. Summarize yesterday: sales, conversion, AOV, units per transaction, traffic, and target variance.
2. List today priorities: target, expected traffic, campaign/promos, staffing coverage, and replenishment focus.
3. Flag stock or operational blockers: low sizes, missing best sellers, repairs, fixtures, queue risk, or POS issues.
4. Add one behavior focus for the team, tied to conversion or service quality.

## Output

- Yesterday recap
- Today target
- Focus products/categories
- Risks
- Team action

## Guardrails

- Do not overwhelm the team with dashboards.
- Make priorities executable in one shift.

## How to adapt

Replace metrics with your POS/traffic system and local store operating rhythm.
